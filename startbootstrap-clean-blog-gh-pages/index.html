College HelpDesk ChatBot

An AI agent providing Customer and Navigation Support

Abstract

In today's educational institutions, providing efficient and timely support to students, faculty, staff and visitors is crucial for fostering a user-centric environment. Traditional methods of assistance often lead to delays and frustration due to limited availability and resources. The "AI Based College Help Desk Chatbot" is an intelligent solution designed to enhance support within educational institutions. The proposed chatbot leverages natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning techniques to understand and respond to user inquiries effectively. The chatbot's responses are generated based on a combination of predefined templates, rule-based logic, and dynamically learned patterns from user interactions.

ChatBot Aims And Objectives

To create Userfriendly Interface which is Accessible 24/7

To develop a database where all the related data is stored and matched with the questions when question is raised

To provide information about college through the chatbot

To resolve user queries and issues during the first interaction with the chatbot

To develop a chatbot that gives intelligent and accurate answers
